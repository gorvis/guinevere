Privacy Policy for Popfaction
Last updated: March 1, 2026

Overview
Popfaction ("the Game") is developed by Smashing Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play Popfaction.

The short version: we collect nothing. Zero. Nada.

Information We Collect
We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or data of any kind. This includes:

No name, email address, or contact information
No device identifiers or advertising IDs
No location data
No usage analytics or crash reports
No gameplay statistics
No purchases or financial data
Data Stored on Your Device
The only data the Game stores is your top score, saved locally on your device using Apple's standard app storage. This data:

Never leaves your device
Is never transmitted to us or any third party
Can be removed by deleting the app
Third-Party Services
Popfaction does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy
Popfaction does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The Game is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy
If we ever update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Any future version that does collect data will require your explicit consent.
